## ğŸš€ Getting Started

### 1. Download
Grab the latest version of the tracker from the [Releases](https://github.com/Xcaptainind/Daily-Habits/releases) page.

### 2. Setup
- Open `Habit_Tracker.xlsx` in **Microsoft Excel** (Desktop version recommended).
- Go to the **Settings/Habits** tab and enter your custom goals.

### 3. Track & Win
- Put an `x` or `1` in the daily boxes to mark a habit as complete.
- Watch the **Dashboard** tab to see your progress bars and consistency charts grow.
